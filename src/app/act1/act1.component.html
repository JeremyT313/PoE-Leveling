Act 1

  • Run pass eveything
  • Kill Hillock (should be an easy kill)
  • Buy Spark from Nessa, link it with Arcane Surge and throw away Fireball
  • Look for B-B-B and type NN for runner's boots
  • Run pass everything and grab WP
  • In Mud Flats only Kill big Packs
  • Grab the 3 nests in Mud Flats then move on to Submerged Passage
  • Grab WP then head to Tidal Island
  • Kill Hailrake
  • Grab QuickSilver and lesser poison support
  • Trade 3 small HP pots for a medium HP pot check for color links and boots
  • Make sure to open a TP in Submerged Passage to go back into later on
  • Try not to be Level 4 in Submerged Passage
  • The Climb only kill decent Packs and help Navali
  • Lower Prison grab WP and go back to town and go into your Submerged Passage TP
  • Go into Submerged TP and enter The Flooded Depths (usually to the left)
  • Kill Dweler of the Deep and logout
  • Get Orb of Storms, Frostblink and Passive Skillpoint in town
  • Set Orb of Storms to "attack without moving" (left-click icon in skillbar)
  • Get Added Lightning Damage in town and link it to Spark + Arcane Surge
  • Trade Spectral throw and iron ring for Topaz Ring
  • Craft two wands with this recipe: Magic-rarity Wand + Topaz Ring + Orb of Alteration
  • If needed, the recipe for Topaz Rings is Iron Ring + Green Skill Gem
  • Start looking out for 2x Sapphire Rings
  • If needed, the recipe for Sapphire Rings is Iron Ring + Blue Skill Gem
  • Grab Lightning Walker in the Passive tree then to Elemental Overload
  • Complete Trial of Ascendancy in Lower Prison
  • Kill Brutus, then logout and get Clarity (keep it Lv5) and Flame Dash in town
  • Replace Frostblink with Flame Dash and set it to "attack without moving"
  • Progress to Ship Graveyard and get the Waypoint
  • Look for Ship Graveyard Cave, get Allflame then get Cavern of Wrath Waypoint
  • Travel to Ship Graveyard Waypoint and kill Fairgraves
  • Go to town to get Passive Skillpoint and Arc
  • Replace Spark with Arc and set it to "attack without moving"
  • Equip Sapphire Rings and kill Merveil
